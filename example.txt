FBI Special Agent Clarice Starling has been assigned to another mission. While on the job, she receives a letter from Hannibal Lecter... After escaping from the detention center, he again commits horrific murders far beyond the borders of the United States. But one of his victims, Mason Verger, decides to organize a hunt for Hannibal himself. Mason craves bloody revenge, dreaming of feeding Lecter alive to pigs. But a victim is always a victim, even in hunting gear. Will the killer finally pay for what he's done this time?
